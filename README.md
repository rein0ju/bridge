# 🌉 Bridge

<p align="center">
  <b>First semester made simple for international students</b>
</p>

---

## 🚀 Overview

Bridge is a platform designed to help international students navigate their first semester in the U.S. with clarity and confidence.

---

## 🌍 Problem

- Too much scattered information  
- No clear starting point  
- High stress during onboarding  

---

## 💡 Solution

Bridge provides a structured and intuitive onboarding experience:
- Step-by-step guidance  
- Centralized resources  
- Clear priorities
